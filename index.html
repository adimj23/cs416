Stat Definitions

Offensive Rating (ORtg): Measures the number of points scored per 100 possessions. A higher ORtg indicates a more efficient offense.

Pace: Represents the number of possessions per game. A higher pace indicates a faster game with more opportunities for scoring.

3-Point Attempt Rate (3PAr): The proportion of a team's field goal attempts that are three-pointers. A higher value indicates a greater emphasis on three-point shooting.

True Shooting Percentage (TS%): Measures a player's or team's scoring efficiency by considering field goals, three-pointers, and free throws. A higher TS% means better overall shooting efficiency.
